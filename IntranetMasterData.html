THE SERVICES WE OFFER

For RHI Magnesita, exact information on master data of customers, vendors and materials is indispensable. As sources of data increase, managing it consistently and keeping it up to date is a complex task.

We have several hubs and teams to keep record of our master data. To make sure everyone uses the same information, all colleagues can and should make use of the Master Data Services offered by these teams. Therefore, we want to provide an oversight of the services scope and where you can turn to if you require specific services.
